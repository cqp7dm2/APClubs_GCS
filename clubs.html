About Us

Due to the covid-19 pandemic started from 2019, the whole world has been affected by the Covid-19 epidemic that caused lockdown, people cannot go out and need to maintain social distancing. Also, due to this pandemic, a lot of people has lost their job especially the fresh graduate as they are still novice to the workplace. Most of the company does not willing to spend their money to train new workers during this pandemic. This situation causes many fresh graduates feel hard to find job during this pandemic. Because of all that reason, our group got inspiration to solve all of the problem that help candidates to get job with just one click.

WHY CHOOSE HIREME ?

This website is designed to advertise the company's empty position and candidates can find their job here. The project comes with a job available tab, urgent hiring tab, view list resume, company profile and user profile as well. Companies can promote their available job and candidates can find a suitable job for themselves following the condition and requirement of the company. The candidate can let the company to download their own resume for further use.
