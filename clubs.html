BADMINTON CLUBS

A Clubs that focus on Badminton Sports for Badminton Lovers

About Us / Description of Clubs

Badminton Clubs was founded in year of 2014 since then there was 45 badminton players in the clubs that sharing the common interested as well as gathering together to learn and sharing about the knowledge of badminton with each other. Badminton clubs also represent APU tow on several awards and tournament in the district and state by year of 2020 and become the most active sports clubs in Asia Pacific University of Technology and Innovation.
